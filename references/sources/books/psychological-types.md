This is a Rare book for use only in the Library. Hardbound; xxii + 654 pp., including bibliographic references in footnotes and an index. This material was first published in German as "Psychologische Typen" by Rascher Verlag, Zurich, 1921. The first English translation by H.G. Baynes was published under the title "Psychological Types" in 1923 by Kegan Paul, London, and Harcourt, Brace and Co., New York. A substantial revision of this material was subsequently published under the same title as Volume 6 of the Collected Works of C.G. Jung, which is also among the Library's holdings.
