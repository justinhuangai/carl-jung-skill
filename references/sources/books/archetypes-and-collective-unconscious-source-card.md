User Notes:

This is a Reference book for use only in the Library. Hardbound; xii + 451 pp., including bibliographic references in footnotes, 60 color and black-and-white illustrations, an appendix, a bibliography, and an index. The first edition of CW 9/i was published in 1959; the second edition was published in 1969. The 1969, second edition is the terminal version of CW 9/i, as reflected in Princeton's digital edition of the Collected Works, which is also among the Library's holdings. Vol. 9 of the Collected Works was published in two parts (of which this is the first), both of which constitute the eighth volume to be published. Publication histories of the 12 principal papers and the one appended item that are included in CW 9/i are provided in the table of contents, footnotes to the text, editorial notes, and the translator's note. Notably, earlier versions of the following three papers were first published in English under the title "The Integration of the Personality" (translated by Stanley Dell; Farrar & Rhinehart, Inc.; New York; 1939), copies of which are among the Library's holdings: • "Archetypes of the Collective Unconscious" (previously published under the same title) • "Conscious, Unconscious, and Individuation" (previously published as "The Meaning of Individuation") • "A Study in the Process of Individuation" (previously published under the same title) Contents: Editorial note Editorial note to the second edition Translator's note List of illustrations I: Archetypes of the collective unconscious The concept of the collective unconscious Concerning the archetypes, with special reference to the anima concept II: Psychological aspects of the mother archetype 1.
